My name is Suhaas and I'm a data scientist and product lead living in NYC. My interests are fairly varied but recently I've been particularly engaged by problems in urban design and in environmental engineering. I think there are countless opportunities to make a difference in climate-related issues and technologies and I hope to explore some of those topics in the various small posts here.

Outside of work and academic interests, I am an avid photographer and dancer. I've been making photos for almost 10 years now and my work has run the gamut from portraits to landscapes, and many things in between. I find the most joy in documenting the quiet moments between the hubbub of ordinary life.

I've also been dancing some form of hip-hop-inspired dance for the last 7 years and have recently become much more interested in house dancing. You can catch me taking dance classes in NYC fairly regularly, and I'm always down to move to music! I'll be sharing some of the music that has recently caught my ear, as well as music from throughout my life.
